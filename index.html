EPISODE IV

A NEW HOPE

It is a dark time for all the lecturers and students in the galaxy. Although the semester is ending, many students do not know anything about their courses. Since students are in trouble, they complain and some of them attempt to join the dark side for a better education. wrong choices and chaos are everywhere.

But there is a light incoming which can bring a better way to educate all the students across the galaxy. And only one has the power and will. He educates the students in such a way that all of them are special and waiting to be discovered. He is a master. He is wise.

HE IS THE GALAXY'S LAST HOPE.
